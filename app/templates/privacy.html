BZCC GameWatch Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: today

We use Steam Sign-In so you never create a password with us. Steam handles authentication; we receive a signed confirmation of your Steam account and store only a minimal public identifier.

What we store

  • SteamID64
  • Public profile link, optional display name, optional avatar URL
  • A session cookie for your login session

What we do not store

  • No passwords, email addresses, payment info, or private Steam data
  • No third‑party trackers or behavioral ads

Game data

Game session data comes from the public RakNet lobby server and is not private. If you sign in, we may link your Steam ID to sessions to show avatars/nicknames. This is optional and reversible.

Your choices

  • Sign out anytime
  • Request unlink/deletion of your identity record

Questions? Contact the site operator.
